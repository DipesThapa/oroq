Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-17. SafeBrowse is operated by CyberHeroez CIC (UK).

What we see

When your device is configured to use SafeBrowse DNS, every DNS query (the domain name being looked up) is sent over an encrypted HTTPS channel to one of our Cloudflare Workers. We use only the domain name to decide whether the query should be allowed or blocked.

What we never see

  • The pages you visit (we don't see URLs, only domains)
  • The content of any page, message, photo, or video
  • Your account passwords or banking details
  • Anything inside encrypted apps (WhatsApp, Snapchat, etc.)

What we store

We keep only aggregate, anonymous statistics for 24 hours:

  • Total query and block counts per filter level per day
  • The top 100 blocked domains per day (popularity counts, not who asked)
  • An approximate count of unique devices, calculated from truncated IP hashes that are rotated every 24 hours and never retained beyond that window

What we never store

  • Per-device query history
  • The mapping between a person and the domains they queried
  • Your IP address (only the daily-rotated, truncated hash exists, and only for the day)

Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Because we don't store anything that identifies you, we cannot produce a personal-data export for you. You can stop using SafeBrowse at any time by removing the profile (iOS) or clearing the Private DNS hostname (Android).
