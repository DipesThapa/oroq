## Full description (max 4000)
```
OroQ helps parents keep children safer online — without spying on them.

See risk. Act with confidence.

Set up filtering and screen-time on your child's phone, then see what matters from your own. OroQ filters harmful websites on the device itself, so your child's browsing is never sent to us. The activity you see as a parent is end-to-end encrypted — even OroQ's servers can't read it.

WHAT PARENTS GET
• Cyber Confidence score — an at-a-glance read on how protected your family is.
• Instant alerts — get notified within seconds when a child is blocked from a phishing, malware, or adult site.
• Web filtering — block harmful categories (adult, malware, phishing, gambling and more) at the DNS level on the device.
• Safe Search & YouTube Restricted Mode — enforced automatically.
• App blocking & screen-time limits — choose which apps to block and set daily limits.
• One dashboard — protection status, blocked threats, screen time and devices, all in one place.

BUILT PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• Filtering runs on the child's device — browsing history and page content never leave it.
• Only the domain of a blocked site is recorded — never full URLs or page content.
• Activity summaries are end-to-end encrypted to the parent's device.
• No advertising, no analytics, no data brokers. No hidden monitoring — the child's phone always shows OroQ is active.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Install OroQ on both phones.
2. On the child's phone, choose "This is my child's phone" and pair using the code (or QR) from your phone.
3. Allow protection. Done — filtering is on and you'll see activity from your phone.

FOR FAMILIES AND SCHOOLS
OroQ is designed around UK safeguarding expectations (KCSIE) and the Age Appropriate Design Code: data is minimised, child screens collect nothing beyond what your settings need, and everything is transparent to the child.

Sign in with Google or a one-time email code — no passwords.

OroQ is built and maintained by CyberHeroez CIC.
```
